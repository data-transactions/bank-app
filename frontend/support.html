Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my transfers?

All outgoing and incoming transfers are visible in the "Recent Transactions" section of your dashboard. You can click on any transaction to view a detailed receipt and tracking ID.

How do notifications work?

NEXA provides real-time alerts for all account activity. You'll see a notification badge in your dashboard header whenever you receive a deposit, complete a transfer, or get a response to a support ticket.

How do I check the status of my support ticket?

You can view all your open and resolved tickets in the "Support" section of your dashboard. We also send you an in-app notification the moment a specialist responds to your inquiry.
